The App

Built for how piping is actually learned

Most learning apps are built for generic music education. The Ceòlmhor app is built specifically for the Highland bagpipe — structured around the four-tier curriculum, the terminology of the tradition, and the particular demands of learning an instrument that cannot be separated from the culture it carries.

Progress is tracked against the specific milestones of each tier. Lesson notes and written feedback are archived and searchable. Practice sessions are logged. Sheet music and practice resources are accessible in one place. The app does not replace lessons — it makes every lesson more productive.

  • Progress Tracking Milestones mapped to the four-tier curriculum
  • Lesson Archive Notes and written feedback from every session
  • Practice Log Session diary with duration and focus tracking
  • Resource Library Sheet music, practice backing tracks, and reference recordings
