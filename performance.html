Process

From enquiry to confirmed booking.

1

Enquiry

Submit details via the contact form. Include your performance date, type, and location.

2

Quote

A written quote is issued within 24 hours. Valid for 14 days from the date of issue.

3

Contract & Deposit

A written contract is issued. A 50% non-refundable deposit secures the date.

4

Confirmed

Your date is confirmed once contract is signed and deposit received. Remaining balance due 7 days before the performance.

Booking Terms
  • 50% non-refundable deposit required within 7 days of confirmation to secure the date
  • Remaining balance due 7 days before the performance.
  • Travel surcharge: £0.50 per mile beyond the first five miles
  • No performance without a signed contract and deposit received
