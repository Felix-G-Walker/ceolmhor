Custom Engagement

Every occasion is different. If your event does not fit neatly into a standard category — or if you have something specific in mind — Ceòlmhor is happy to discuss a bespoke arrangement. Pricing is agreed individually based on the nature, duration, and location of the engagement.

Venues, Institutions & Formal Events

Ceòlmhor works with event teams who need a reliable piper for formal, private and public-facing occasions. This includes hotels, private venues, universities, schools, councils, cultural organisations, charities and corporate hosts.

Performances can be arranged for guest arrivals, drinks receptions, ceremonies, formal welcomes, dinner entrances, Burns Night addresses and civic moments. Timings, dress, repertoire and practical requirements are agreed in advance, with clear written terms before the booking is confirmed.

Enquire about a venue or institutional booking

Based in Edinburgh. Available across Scotland. A travel surcharge of £0.50 per mile applies beyond the first five miles. Payment links are provided with your booking confirmation — no payment is required to enquire.

  • 50% non-refundable deposit required within 7 days of confirmation to secure the date
