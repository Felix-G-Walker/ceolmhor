Graduation & Civic Ceremony

Marking the transition from candidate to graduate.

Graduation ceremonies and civic occasions carry a formality that rewards ceremony. A piper marks the transition — from candidate to graduate, from private to public — with a sound that is both ancient and immediately understood. Ceòlmhor provides piping for university graduations, civic receptions, mayoral and council ceremonies, and formal institutional events across Edinburgh and Scotland.

Ceremony Piper

£175

  • Processional music as graduates enter
  • Piping during any formal procession within the ceremony
  • Recessional

Covers the core ceremonial role. Suited to institutions requiring piping within the formal proceedings only.

Standard Package

Full Ceremony Piper

£250

  • Everything in the Ceremony Piper

Recommended for institutions and civic bodies where the piper plays a role before, during, and after the formal ceremony.
