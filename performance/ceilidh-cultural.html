Ceilidh & Cultural Events

Traditional piping performed with knowledge of the tradition behind it.

From Highland games to cultural festivals, community gatherings to civic celebrations, Ceòlmhor is available for public-facing performance across Scotland and beyond. The repertoire spans marches, airs, jigs, reels, and Pìobaireachd — each piece selected and performed with an understanding of what it is and where it comes from.

Base Package

Single Set

£150

  • One full performance set — marches, airs, jigs, and reels as appropriate
  • Approximately 20–30 minutes of performance

Suited to shorter slots, opening performances, and events where a single focused set is required.

Standard Package

Extended Presence

£200

  • Two performance sets with interval
  • Approximately 45–60 minutes of total performance
  • Suitable for extended events, stage slots, and Highland games appearances

For events that benefit from a longer or more varied piping presence. Multi-session and full-day engagements are priced individually on enquiry.

Ceilidh

Ceilidh & Dance Evening

£250

  • Opening piping as guests arrive and the evening begins
  • Sets of marches, jigs, reels, and strathspeys suited to ceilidh dancing
  • Coordination with any band or caller present
  • Closing piece to formally end the evening

For ceilidh nights, Highland balls, and informal dance evenings where the pipes provide energy, momentum, and a distinctly Scottish atmosphere. Repertoire drawn from the dance tradition — lively, varied, and performed with the occasion in mind.

Booking Terms
  • 50% non-refundable deposit required to secure the date
  • Remaining balance due 7 days before the event
  • Multi-session and full-day engagements priced individually on enquiry
  • Travel surcharge: £0.50 per mile beyond the first five miles from Edinburgh city centre
  • Written contract issued for every booking
