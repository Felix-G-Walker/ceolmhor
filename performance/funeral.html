Funeral & Memorial

The Highland bagpipe has accompanied the dead for centuries. It is one of the few instruments whose sound is inseparable from the ceremony of loss — and it carries that weight with a dignity that nothing else quite matches.

Ceòlmhor offers funeral and memorial piping across Edinburgh and Scotland. From £125, including arrival piping, a lament, and a recessional. Written confirmation is provided for every booking.

Funeral and memorial services are priced below our standard rate by intention. Grief is not an occasion for commercial calculation, and we have no interest in making it one. Ceòlmhor believes that the pipes should be accessible to every family that wants them at a moment of loss — not only to those for whom cost is no consideration.
