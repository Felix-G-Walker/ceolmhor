Corporate & Business Dinner

An evening that has been designed, not assembled.

A piper at a corporate dinner signals something deliberate — that the occasion has been considered, that the evening has been given shape. Ceòlmhor provides professional piping for corporate dinners, awards evenings, product launches, and private receptions. Written terms are provided before any booking is confirmed.

Base Package

Reception Piper

£200

  • Arrival piping as guests are welcomed
  • A formal entrance piece
  • One set piece during the reception

Suitable for events where a piping presence at arrival and during the reception is the primary requirement. Includes professional execution of the defined ceremonial moments. Standard repertoire appropriate to the occasion. No advance consultation beyond logistics.

Standard Package

Dinner Piper

£275

  • Everything in the Reception Piper
  • Background piping during pre-dinner drinks
  • Grand entrance for the top table or award recipients
  • Pre-event repertoire conversation to incorporate preferences and coordinate with the event running order and any masters of ceremonies

Suited to formal dinners and awards evenings where the piper plays a structured role in the running of the event. Includes a pre-event repertoire conversation — an opportunity to indicate preferences, request specific tunes, or flag anything of cultural or personal significance to the occasion.

Premium Package

Full Evening Piper

£350

  • Everything in the Dinner Piper
  • Personalised set piece arranged for the occasion
  • Dedicated pre-event consultation and written programme note prepared for the occasion
  • Extended evening piping across multiple moments

For high-profile events where the piper is a consistent and visible presence throughout the evening. Includes a dedicated pre-event consultation by phone or email, and a personalised programme prepared specifically for the occasion. A written programme note explaining the repertoire choices is provided to the host.

Additional Performance Set

£75

An additional performance set of approximately 20–25 minutes. Can be added to any package at the time of booking or on request. Repertoire appropriate to the occasion.

Booking Terms
  • 50% non-refundable deposit required to secure the date
  • Remaining balance due 7 days before the event
  • Travel surcharge: £0.50 per mile beyond the first five miles from Edinburgh city centre
  • Written contract issued for every booking
