Felix Walker is an Edinburgh-based piper with a career spanning competition, ceremony, and international performance. A performer with the Royal Edinburgh Military Tattoo and Lone Piper at the Armistice Day Service at the Scottish National War Memorial, he offers structured tuition from first chanter to competition standard, and professional performance for private, corporate, and institutional occasions across Scotland and beyond.

Tuition

Four progressive tiers, from the first notes on the practice chanter through to competition-standard Pìobaireachd. Lessons are structured, cumulative, and taught in Edinburgh or online worldwide.
