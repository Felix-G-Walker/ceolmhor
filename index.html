Piping Tuition & Performance Across Scotland

Ceòlmhor, meaning resonant or melodious in Scottish Gaelic, is built around the depth of the tradition it promotes. Ceòlmhor offers advanced piping tuition and professional performance services, based in Edinburgh and available worldwide. Structured four-tier instruction takes students from first chanter to competition standard, while professional piping is available for weddings, funerals, and events as requested.
